Why Join Us?

A car club is a community of automotive enthusiasts who share a common interest in a general or specific manufacturer of car. Some car clubs are broken down by an appreciation for a particular manufacturer or just a general type of car such as muscle cars or sports cars. Across the world, enthusiasts gather to share and discuss their favorite cars withers in a nearby vicinity. The clubs often host regular meetings and events that may discuss a particular topic or it may just be a show and shine, which is a great way to clean and show off your car!

Confidence in Motion

Suspension

The WRX STI has a 4-wheel independent suspension with aluminium front lower control arms, plus double wishbone rear suspension.

Engine

The EJ25 is battle-tested and race-proven. The 2.5-liter, turbocharged engine with 305 bhp & 290 lb-ft of torque is certain to make you smile.

Brakes

There's a reason why the name Brembo demands such respect. Case in point: the braking system of the STI.  Powerful cross drilled & ventilated.

Transmission

The venerable 6-speed manual of the STi is a work of mechanical art. With impeccable street cred & a bulletproof reputation, ultimate control.
